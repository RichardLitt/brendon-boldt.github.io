Brendon Boldt

I am a Master of Language Technology student at Carnegie Mellon University's Language Technology Institute. My primary interests lie in artificial emergent languages. Phrased as a series of questions,

  • What kind of language can machine learning-equipped agents invent in order to cooperate on a task?
  • What attributes in the environment correspond to attributes in the emergent language?
  • Instead of starting with a fully-formed language and building up an agent's proficiency in it, can we build up the agent's proficiency as the language is developed from scratch?
